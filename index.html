Common Questions

How often should I use these products?
BotaniButter works best when applied to damp hair after washing, or as needed for moisture. The Herbal Hair Oil can be used 2-3 times weekly for scalp massages or daily for sealing ends.
Are these products suitable for all hair types?
Yes! Our products are specially formulated for all textures of natural African hair - from type 3 curls to type 4 coils and kinks. Even transitioning hair sees great results.
How long does shipping take?
We process orders within 1-2 business days. Lagos deliveries typically arrive in 2-3 days, while other states take 3-5 business days via our trusted delivery partners.
Are your products organic?
While we use the highest quality natural ingredients, we're not certified organic. However, all our butters and oils are unrefined and our herbs are carefully sourced for purity and potency.
Can I use both products together?
Absolutely! For best results, apply the Herbal Hair Oil to your scalp and BotaniButter to your lengths and ends. They work synergistically to nourish from root to tip.
